Earthquake Safety Checklist

Save Extra Flashlights, with spare batteries.

Keep a first aid kit, save a first aid book and have members of your household take basic CPR courses, and know basic first aid

Keep fire estinguishers near, in case of a fire.

Keep an emergency route in case of an earthquake, keep fire escapes near and have a good building structure.

Are your staff regularly trained in fire safety matters?

Keep extra food and water, make sure that they arent expired or have 6 months extra after the expiration date.



For more fire safety or any questions feel free to contact us.
